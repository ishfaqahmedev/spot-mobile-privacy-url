Privacy Policy for Spot

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Introduction

Spot ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application that allows users to create and share photo memories tied to locations. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Camera: We access your device camera to let you take photos for memories. Photos are only captured when you choose to take them.

Photo Library: We access your photo library to let you select existing photos for memories.

Location: We collect your location to associate memories with places and to show nearby memories. Location is only used while the app is in use or with your explicit permission.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide app functionality: creating memories, displaying them on a map, and notifying you of nearby memories. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage

Your photos and location data are stored securely on our servers to provide the app's features. You can delete your data at any time by deleting your account within the app.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services (such as cloud hosting and analytics) that have their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Spot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at spotapp.contact@gmail.com.
